Legal Team
  • Anna Demidchik Esq. Super Lawyers Rising Stars
    Anna Demidchik is the founding attorney of Demidchik Law Firm. She focuses primarily on immigration and criminal law. Anna received her J.D. from Hofstra Law (Maurice A. Dean School of Law). She is admitted to practice law in New York, California, Pennsylvania, Washington State, Wisconsin. Besides English, Anna speaks Russian and Mandarin.
  • Jason Molesso Esq.
    Jason C. Molesso is licensed to practice Law in the States of New York and Texas, as well as several Federal Courts, including the Court of Federal Claims in Washington, D.C. Jason has exclusively represented injured people in Medical Malpractice, Medical Device and Pharmaceutical, Vaccine, Construction, Premises Liability, Automobile, and General Negligence cases throughout the United States. In almost 20 years of practice, Jason has deposed over 1000 medical and scientific witnesses, has taken more than 40 trial verdicts, and has argued in excess of 20 appeals; all while helping injured people receive the Justice they deserve.
  • Shuyang Xie Esq.
    Shuyang Xie obtained her Juris Doctorate from Tulane Law School. She earned a Master of Public Administration from Cornell University. Currently, she is admitted to practice in the state courts of New York and New Jersey. Her practice focuses primarily on immigration law and civil litigation. Her immigration work includes family-based immigration, employment-based visas and removal proceedings. Her civil work experience includes handling landlord and tenant cases, contract disputes, federal wage-and-hour litigations, contested and uncontested divorce, and family court matters.
  • Robert S. Hazzard, Esq.
    Robert S. Hazzard graduated Cum Laude from St. John's University with a B.S. in Criminal Justice. After graduation, he obtained his Juris Doctorate from Touro Law School and upon his admission, became an associate for a General Practice law firm where he practiced both criminal and civil litigation from 2000 until 2008, successfully trying many cases. Thereafter, from 2008 to 2013, Mr. Hazzard was General Counsel for an insurance company handling all of its corporate affairs, litigation and regulatory compliance. From 2013 to 2018, Robert was the Legal Director of an international developer where he handled all corporate and contractual matters, multi-million dollar project financing, equity investments, and external managed advisory.
  • Michael Brown Esq.
    Michael Brown, Esquire has been an attorney in the Orlando area for over 8 years and Of Counsel at Demidchik Law Firm. He was a prosecutor for over 5 years with the State Attorney's Office in Orange and Osceola County. There, he handled misdemeanor cases, juvenile cases, felony cases, felony violations of probation, and litigated over 20 jury trials. Mr. Brown left the State Attorney's Office to become a civil litigator in the areas of foreclosure, breaches of contract, and insurance defense. He has practiced in Courts throughout the State. Mr. Brown is licensed to practice in all courts in the state of Florida. He is a member of the Florida Bar and the 3 Florida Federal Courts.
  • Yunchao (Paul) Song Esq.
    Yunchao (Paul) Song is licensed to practice law in California, Texas, and New York. He is admitted to the US Federal Court in the Eastern District, Northern District, and Central District of California. He graduated from University of Southern California, Gould School of Law, where he received Master of Laws and Juris Doctor degrees. Mr. Song is currently practicing immigration law, family law, and criminal law. He has extensive experience in these fields. Mr. Song is passionate about immigration, legal service, and protecting immigrants' legal rights. He is recognized by his clients for his professionalism and patience.
  • Yuan (Bill) Li Esq.
    Yuan Li is licensed to practice in California and is admitted to the bar of the US Federal Court in the Central District of California. He is also a licensed attorney in China, and has several years of experience of legal practice there. He is familiar with the laws of China and the United States, and has extensive litigation experience. In addition to criminal and civil litigation, he has done substantial research on corporate governance, Anti-Unfair Competition Law of China, Intellectual Property Law, government supervision, and other business areas. Since he started his law practice in 2010, he has worked in the Shanghai office of the Dentons law firm and in the major government economic oversight departments in Shanghai. He is now committed to dedicating himself to exclusive litigation and other legal services to the Chinese community in California.
  • Michael Walsh Esq. Super Lawyers
    Michael Walsh brings over 40 years litigation experience to the firm. He specializes in criminal defense litigation both at the trial and appellate level in both State and Federal Courts. He has been selected again as a Super Lawyer in the Criminal Defense field.

    His practice includes representation of individuals who are charged with the most serious of cases. .He has represented clients in Federal cases and investigations in numerous jurisdictions including New York, Massachusetts, Maryland, Michigan, California, Oregon, Hawaii, Alabama and all districts for Illinois.

    He is licensed in the State of Illinois and was a former member of the Illinois Capital Litigation Bar Panel. He has been admitted to practice in the United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, where he is a member of its Trial Bar. He has also been admitted as a regular and pro-vice member in numerous US district and state courts throughout the United States. He has been admitted to practice in the Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit.

    Mr. Walsh obtained his law degree in 1980 from De Paul University School of Law. He graduated from Dartmouth College in 1977 with an A.B. in History.
  • Howard Cohn Esq.
    Howard brings to the Firm over 45 years of legal and trial experience. A former Judge Pro Team of the Alameda County Municipal Court, Howard has presided over hundreds of criminal and civil trials. Howard is a former trustee and general counsel of one of the largest Real Estate Investment Trusts in the US, owning and managing over 6 million square feet of commercial property.

    For the past 25 years, Howard has been the general counsel of a Software company who is a leader in producing software for the real estate mortgage industry. Howard's broad experience includes Family Law, Wills and Trusts, Business Disputes and Litigation, and Criminal Law. A collegiate boxer and baseball player, Howard has excelled in athletic as well as legal competition. Howard has three grown children. When away from the office Howard enjoys poker and golf.
  • James Berdelle Esq.
    James is known for the quality of his achievements on behalf of clients in the litigation and trial of highly complex matters. James honed his litigation and trial skills during his legal career while working for a number of large law firms in the United States. His legal work has extended to countless state and federal courts across the land. James has successfully managed the litigation and trial of large complicated and complex commercial claims, class action claims, business and contract dispute claims, personal injury claims, property damage claims, and employment-related claims. His representative accomplishments include acting as lead trial counsel in:

    • a large shareholder derivative class action claim saving client from paying multiple millions and from unwinding of merger;
    • many large commercial, contract, and business dispute claims saving clients from paying multiple millions;
    • a Medicare-related cost recovery class action claim securing dismissal on behalf of client;
    • a large commercial disparagement claim saving client from paying multiple millions;
    • many large environmental claims saving clients from paying multiple millions;
    • many employment-related claims on behalf of both employees and employers, including securing TRO against renegade chief employee; and
    • an intellectual property and commercial disparagement claim saving client from paying millions.

    James has now brought his attorney skills to Demidchik Law Firm and to the firm's valued clients.
  • Chen (Leo) Zhao Esq.
    Leo Zhao obtained his Master of Laws degree from the University of Minnesota Law School in 2018. He is licensed to practice law in New York. His primary focus is on immigration law, which is Federal and therefore can be practiced in any state regardless of the license. Leo has achieved notable success in assisting his clients in obtaining lawful permanent resident status (green card status), asylee status, citizenship, and other immigration benefits. Committed to promoting humanity and justice, Leo is dedicated to helping immigrants navigate the legal system and leveraging his fluency in both Chinese and English as a bilingual attorney. In addition to his professionalism, Leo also showcases remarkable talent as a singer and performer.
  • Yibo Wang
    Yibo Wang, an accomplished legal professional, holds a Bachelor of Laws degree from the prestigious Central South University of Economics and Law. She further honed her expertise through her graduate studies at Boston University School of Law and is duly licensed to practice law in both China and New York State. Wang's unique advantage lies in her proficiency in multiple languages, including Mandarin, Shanghainese, and English, allowing her to effectively serve a diverse clientele. Notably, she is widely acknowledged for her exceptional patience and unwavering sense of responsibility, traits that shine through in her delivery of professional services to clients. With a commitment to excellence, Yibo Wang remains a trusted and sought-after legal counsel.
  • Nicole  Tang
    Nicole is an accomplished corporate and immigration attorney, licensed to practice in New York and California. Her linguistic capabilities encompass fluency in Mandarin, Cantonese, and English. Her specialization concentrates primarily on corporate practice, such as entity formation, corporate governance, corporate finance, mergers and acquisitions, and commercial transactions; and business immigration and global mobility, including EB1, EB2 NIW, EB2/3 PERM, H-1B, O-1, L-1, among others.

    With extensive experience in corporate and immigration law, she excels in providing client-centered legal consultations and tailoring immigration solutions to meet the specific needs of each company and foreign employee.
